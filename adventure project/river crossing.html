River crossing is synonymous with fun! This adrenaline-pumped watersport is one of the most daring and thrilling adventure activities in Himachal, wherein a rope is tied across a river on the two river banks and you have to cross the river using a safety harness that is attached to the rope. This water activity can get challenging as water flows in full force underneath and demands great balancing skills. River crossing is one of the most loved activities in Himachal Pradesh that draw tourists from all over the world.
