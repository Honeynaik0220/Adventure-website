Combine snowboarding with the natural virgin slopes of Himachal Pradesh, and you cannot go wrong!!! Snowboarding is a much younger sport than skiing, which allows easy floating on soft virgin snow. This winter sport involves riding on a board that is attached to the feet of the rider by snowboarding boot. Snowboarding was inspired from wave surfing. It is no secret that Himachal Pradesh is home to some of the best slopes in the skiing and snowboarding world. Some of these slopes are ranked the best in offering excellent skiing experience and others for their breathtaking views and beauty. While not boasting the mega-resorts of New Zealand or Europe, Himachal Pradesh offers you a unique snowboarding experience. Snowboarders will find plenty of good rides at all the ski areas.
